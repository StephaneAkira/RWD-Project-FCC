Steve Jobs

Here's a time line of Steve Jobs's life:

  • 1955 - Born in San Francisco California , U.S
  • 1972 - Attended Reed College before wihdrawing the same Year
  • 1974 - Traveled through India , seeking enlightenment before later studying Zen Buddhism
  • 1976 - Became co-founder of Apple with Wozniak
  • 1985 - Jobs departed Apple after a long power struggle with the company's board and its then-CEO, John Sculley. That same year, Jobs took some Apple employees with him to found NeXT,
  • 1986 - he bought the computer graphics division of Lucasfilm, which was spun off independently as Pixar
  • 1997 - Jobs returned to Apple as CEO after the company's acquisition of NeXT. He was largely responsible for reviving Apple, which was on the verge of bankruptcy.
  • 2003 - Jobs was diagnosed with a pancreatic neuroendocrine tumor.
  • 2011 - He died of tumor-related respiratory arrest
  • 2022 - he was posthumously awarded the Presidential Medal of Freedom. Since his death, he has won 141 patents; Jobs holds over 450 patents in total.
